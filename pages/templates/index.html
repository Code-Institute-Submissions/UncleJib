Welcome to Uncle Jib's

#EnjoyTheRide


Here at Uncle Jib's, we started out in 1953 by a small team of crazy, snow sports obsessed individuals... Also known as The Jibson family.

They saw an opportunity and believed they could start up a unique snowboard shop inside their garage. Nearly 80 year's later, Uncle Jib's has been going from strength to strength and has just recently moved its headquaters to North Yorkshire, UK.

We know that there isn't much snow within the UK, but that doesnt mean there are no snowboards.

All Mountain

Big Mountain

Big Mountain, powder & backcountry snowboards - for the powder hound! You live for riding off-piste in the deep soft white stuff. Think about the type of rider you are and how you will be using your snowboard. If you are not sure - the 'All Mountain' option is the one to go for.

Freestyle

Freeride snowboards are for cruising the pistes and powder, covering the entire resort as quickly as you can. Cutting sharp edges into the piste, then blasting off into the powder at every opportunity, dropping cliffs if you dare but speed rather than air is your thing.

Pipe and Pipe

If you are choosing a board for use in the snow park or half pipe, spins, grabs and kickers are your thing. You'd rather spin 5 than carve hard turns into the piste! You need a specialised snowboard for this kind of riding. The snowboard park is the pace for big kickers and giant air!

Jib and Street

If you ride a jib or street style snowboard, rails and man made obstacles are your playground. You need a short, flexible, twin tip board with soft edges for sliding, jibbing and hitting boxes. Think about the type of rider you are and how you will be using your snowboard.

View Boards

Boardercross

Boardercross snowboards are specialist snowboards designed for racing over the rough and tumble of a boarder cross course. Boardercross snowboards can handle any terrain and can therefore be ridden all mountain but they are not for all riders!
