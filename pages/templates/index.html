Welcome to Uncle Jib's

#EnjoyTheRide


Here at Uncle Jib's, we started out in 1953 by a small team of crazy, snow sports obsessed individuals... Also known as The White's family.

They saw an opportunity and believed they could start up a unique snowboard shop withing their garage. Nearly 80 year's later, Uncle Jib's has been going from strength to strength and has just recently moved its headquaters to North Yorkshire, UK.

We know that there isn't much snow within the UK, but that doesnt mean there are no snowboards.
