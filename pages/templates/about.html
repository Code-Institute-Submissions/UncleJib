Welcome to Uncle Jib's


Heres a little story about us...



The Family you always needed in winter!



Here at Uncle Jib's, we know how much the right snowboard can effect your riding capability. We have picked out and categorised the major 6 styles of snowboarding including...

  • All Mountain
  • Big Mountain
  • Freestyle
  • Park and Pipe
  • Street and Jib
  • BoarderCross

We know that snowboarding makes winter tolerable, not tolerable...enjoyable! Instead of shoveling snow from the driveway, feeling the frostbite on your fingers and nose, we prefer the rush from travelling fast and flying through the air.

For this we know that you need the correct board that matches with your riding style. With a wide variety on site we always try to have at least 12 different types of each riding style, to ensure you have a selection to choose from.

Environmental Features

We understand that our world is being destroyed by Green House Gases(GHGs). To help fight back, we only use sustainable products which have been branded and highlighted to encourage mindful shopping. Within our warehouse, we have Solar PV and thermal generating electriciy and heat provided by the sun to allow. This is just one of the many measures we take to help fight back at GHGs!
